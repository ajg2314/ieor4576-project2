About this agent

This is a multi-agent sector analyst that retrieves real financial data from SEC EDGAR — the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission's public filing database — and performs an automated three-step analysis:

  1. Collect — fetches structured financial data (revenue, income, margins) from XBRL filings and scrapes MD&A sections from 10-K/10-Q documents in real time.
  2. Explore — computes YoY growth rates, operating margins, and sector comparisons; generates charts using Python executed at runtime.
  3. Hypothesize — forms a grounded analyst hypothesis with cited evidence and a full memo.

Scope: Public US companies listed on SEC EDGAR. Ask about individual companies (e.g. NVDA, AAPL) or sectors (semiconductors, cloud, social media). Follow-up questions are supported.
